About us

JMG Clothing Co. Ltd was started in 2018 to manufacture clothes for local and foreign market. We manufacture all type of clothes that are requested by our customers and also large amount of production.

Our clothes is 100% original and man made. We handle the whole supply process from designing, creating of fabrics, the good quality control and to ship delivery in time.

NOTE: We are well experienced and always follow in the fashion and trends for style collections.
